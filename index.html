Why CLOUD?

Smart

All of our clouds are artificially grown in a lab, and designed with special technologies that make them more suitable for storage purposes than land-based storage facilities. Each cloud is self-driving, self-sufficient, and capable of high-speed troposphere-to-ground transportation for delivering and fetching items.

Safe

Our cloud clusters are safely hidden amongst natural clouds. Using the latest ZeusSleepTM technology, our clouds are unable to produce lightning. Your items are securely locked in air-tight, waterproof, temperature-controlled containers. Each cloud is also equipped with a number of security devices to prevent trespassing and cloud-hijacking.

Sustainable

Our artificial clouds are largely composed of hydrogen, helium, and oxygen. They are self-sustaining and produce no harmful emissions. Like normal clouds, our clouds also have natural lifecycles. They take six months to dissipate, but rest assured that once your cloud nears the end of its lifecycle, your items will be safely transferred to a new cloud.
